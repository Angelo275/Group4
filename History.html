Types of Ramen

A wide variety of ramen exists in Japan, with geographical and vendor-specific differences even in varieties that share the same name. Although ramen usually has toppings, ramen can be broadly categorized by its two main ingredients: noodles and broth.

Noddles: Most noodles are made from four basic ingredients: wheat flour, salt, water, and kansui.
Soup: Ramen soup is generally made from stock based on chicken or pork, combined with a variety of ingredients such as pork bones, katsuobushi, niboshi, beef bones, shiitake, onions, and kombu.
